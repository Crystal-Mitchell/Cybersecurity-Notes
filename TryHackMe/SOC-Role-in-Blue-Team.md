# SOC Role in Blue Team

## Date
2026-06-22

## Objective
Learn the role of a SOC analyst and how blue teams defend organizations.

## Key Concepts Learned

- SOC analysts monitor security alerts
- Analysts investigate suspicious activity
- Documentation is important
- Escalation procedures help handle incidents

## What I Learned

The SOC analyst's job is not just to respond to alerts but to investigate, document findings, and determine whether activity is malicious.

## Real World Application

A SOC analyst may review login failures, suspicious processes, or unusual network traffic to identify potential threats.
